Qualifications to Become a Temporary Driver

  • Must be at least 21 years old.
  • Have a valid Kenyan Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) or an equivalent qualification.
  • Hold a valid Certificate of Good Conduct.
  • Have at least 1 year of driving experience.
  • Be physically and mentally fit for the role.
